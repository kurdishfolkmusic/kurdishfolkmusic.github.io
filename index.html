Kurdish Folk Music Corpus

An Effort to Preserve and Analyze the Heritage that Manifests itself in Kurdish Folk Music

About

The transmission of history, especially for the Kurds, has been primarily oral; and music is one of the important tools used by the Kurdish people to narrate stories and document historical events when writing was not accessible.This research project aims to uncover the importance of Kurdish folk music both as historical and linguistic data.

The main output of this project is the corpus of folk songs, which attempts to encompass as many dialects, genres, styles, and modalities (lyrics, audio, music notation, etc.). Moreover, crowdsourcing initiatives will be announced here, and you will be able to contribute to this ambitious project. Contributions from musicologists, historians, linguists, and others who are passionate about the vision of this project are welcome.

To resolve the ambiguity often present in literature on Kurdish folk music, this project will utilize computational and statistical methods to systematically classify and detect patterns within the structure and aesthetics of the songs.

This project will greatly benefit the state of Kurdish music from all aspects, and it will shed light on its historical and cultural significance.
